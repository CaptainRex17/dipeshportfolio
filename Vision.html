Walks in Nature

My go to leisure activity for stress relief is going for a walk. Nature never fails me and I love going out in nature. I love watching the birds go round their business, I love watching squirrels run around chasing each other and I love all the trees and the fresh oxygen.




Reading

Reading is what I do when I want to feel peace. Reading comes natural to me and I cannot imagine a life without books. I love reading philosophy and self-help books. Other than that, I read a mystery or detective novel here and there. I also read fiction but only sometimes because good fiction is hard to come by nowadays. I mostly read old fictional novels and literature.

Poetry is another thing I cannot imagine my life without. There are some fine poems out there in the world and I want to read each and every one of them! A poem for your liking!
